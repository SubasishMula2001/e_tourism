ECO ADVENTURE RESORTS KHAIRABERA
Cradled in the foothills of the Ayodhya hills, alongside a serene water body, the Eco Adventure Resorts at Khairabera is a short drive from Purulia town (67km), West Bengal. The property is an ecologically sensitive and activity oriented nature retreat. The ethos of the resort is rooted in the surroundings and the aim is to make each traveller absorb and reflect the magic of it’s unique location. With no distractions or intrusions of routine life, the natural beauty and rhythm of the land is preserved and one can truly reconnect with nature. Spacious luxury tents with private sit-outs and en-suite bathrooms overlook spectacular views of the dramatic wilderness around. Local festivals such as Palash are a major attraction while the famed Chau dancers enthrall visitors with their performance by the waterside after sunset. Recognised by the Dept of Tourism, India, as the 2nd tented property in the country and with several awards to it’s credit, the Eco Adventure Resort at Khairabera is a must visit. Come feel the oneness with nature! Life is outside…
ECO ADVENTURE RESORTS TEMI BUNGALOW
Tea and tourism is a blend currently brewing in South Sikkim. The Temi Tea Estate lies between Damthang and Temi Bazaar. The Bada Bungalow at Temi, a heritage property and resort overlooking lush green landscapes with spectacular views of snow clad mountains, promises to be a rejuvenating experience. Specially designed tours to Namchi and Ravangla, among others, are available for guests while adventure sports like paragliding add to the thrill. November is time for the cherry blossom festival and a perfect time to visit. Experience tea garden life with luxury accommodation and amenities. And on your way out, pick up some famous premium quality, organic Temi Tea!
